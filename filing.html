Income Tax Return is a form where anassessee files the particulars about this income and tax thereon to Income Tax Department. Income tax is a tax imposed on income of a person by the Central Government Documents required for Income Tax Filing in India PAN card registration Bank statements T.D.S. Certificates in Form 16 or 16A as applicable Investment details Rent receipts for claiming house rent allowance Details of advance tax or self-assessment tax if paid For businesses - a copy each of the audit report, balance sheet, trading, profit and loss account, personal account of proprietor or partners Statement of receipts and payments if books not maintained Receipts of payment such as insurance premium, donations made, new equity shares, mutual fund, medical expenses etc... to claim deductions Type of Income Tax Return Form

ITR 1

The form ITR 1 is for resident individuals with total income not more than Rs. 50 lakhs. Disclosure should beprovided forbreakup of salary and house property.

ITR 2

The form ITR 2 is applicable for individuals and HUFs who are not having income from profits and gains of business or profession.

ITR 3

The Form ITR 3 is for individuals and HUFs having income from profits and gains of business or profession.

ITR 4

The Form ITR 4 is for presumptive income from business and profession.

ITR 5

The Form ITR 5 is for persons other than individual HUF company namely Firms, BOI and AOP

ITR 6

The Form ITR 6 is for companies other than companies claiming exemption under Section 11.

ITR 7

The Form ITR 7 is for person including companies which are required to furnish return under Section 139(4A) or 139(4B) or 139(4C) or 139(4D) or 139(4E) or 139(4F) of the Income Tax Act.
